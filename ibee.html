IBee ?

The I Bee mobile application will allow beekeepers to easily collect, store, and analyze real-time data from their beehives. It will provide a user-friendly interface with a comprehensive overview of the bee colonies' status, including graphs, dashboards, and alerts to highlight trends and potential issues. I Bee will enhance informed decision-making and facilitate proactive hive management, providing beekeepers with a powerful tool to maximize the utilization of data generated by hive sensors.
